01. About

I'm a mechatronics and robotics systems engineer with an M.S. in Computer Science from Santa Clara University and B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from San José State University. From an early age, I've been taking apart things to see how they work phones, consoles, computers, etc., and putting them back together better. That instinct never left.

My work sits at an uncommon intersection: I can architect a distributed ML training run and then flash the BLDC motor firmware that feeds it data. At Shifamed, I joined Laza Medical as the 5th engineering hire for a medtech robot — leading motor control stack feasibility from a blank slate. I thrive in early-stage environments where the hardware and software problem spaces are still being defined.
